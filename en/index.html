What is geomati.co?

geomati.co is a geospatial company specialized on open source software. We create tools for any project that needs the most recent geo technologies, from environmental apps to geospatial analysis for mobility.

  • We like to establish a personalized and honest relation between customers and developers, without intermediaries.
  • We use and make open source code. We know many of the existing geospatial projects out there (PostGIS, OpenLayers, LeafletJS) and we like to contribute to them as much as possible, as we have effectively done with Geotools, GeoServer, gvSIG or QGIS.
  • We believe in collaboration, shared knowledge, honesty and transparency.
  • geomati.co started on the Girona SIG libre conference in March 2011.

The people

  • Fernando González

    During his early years he worked mainly in Java and has contributed to opensource geospatial projects such as gvSIG, gvSIG CE and GeoTools. In the past years his activity has drifted towards the web, using Javascript technologies.

    Always looking for a challenge, he is recently interested in data analysis and artificial intelligence.

  • Óscar Fonts

Since 2010 he continues his career as a freelance developer. He has also done training activities at the Open University of Catalunya (UOC) and the Girona University.

  • Micho García

  • Víctor González

  • Martí Pericay

What do we do

  • Geospatial analysis

    satagro.eu

    Exploitation and analysis of data with geographic component to help our clients in decision making.

    Network analysis, satellite image manipulation, optimal route calculation and statistical analysis are some of the solutions we develop for mobility, logistics and marketing companies.

    Customers: satagro.pl, Progess, Traycco.
    Technologies: PostGIS, QGIS, GDAL, TurfJS.

  • Geoserver and PostGIS training


    We offer basic and advanced PostGIS courses (security, performance, PL/PgSQL) and GeoServer courses (OGC, optimization, raster) to many institutions and companies, as we did for the Universitat de Girona, the Cartographic Intitute of Catalonia, Intergraph, the Barcelona Harbour, Deimos Space, the National University of Costa Rica, or GeoSUR.

    Training: PostgreSQL/PostGIS, GeoServer, QGIS, Vector Tiles, Open Layers, LeafletJS, Geospatial analysis, Spatial Data Infrastructures, High Availability and OGC standards.

  • Environmental applications

    Precision agriculture
    Taxo&Map: biodiversity

    We have implemented land management systems and geographic data portals in many institutions of the environmental or agricultural sector, from local governments to international organizations.

    We rely on the Open Geospatial Consortium international standards and the Spatial Data Infrastructure principles.

    Customers: Museo de Ciencias Naturales de Barcelona, Satagro, Universidad de Barcelona, CSIC.
    Technologies: PostgreSQL/PostGIS, GeoServer, GeoNetwork, OpenLayers, Leaflet.



  • Mobile technologies

    Menorca Offline

    Development of mobile applications based on hybrid solutions with geographic component for both iOS and Android.

    We move the business logic to mobile devices making use of the potential of front-end tools and we integrate them in the applications.

    Visualization through MapboxGL, offline features or geospatial analysis in the device through the use of TurfJS are some of the solutions we have developed.

    Customers: Transports Metropolitans de Barcelona, Espais Escrits.
    Technologies: Apache Cordova, Mapbox GL JS, TurfJS.

  • Customization of existing projects

    We can extend your desktop GIS of choice, either QGIS or gvSIG / gvSIG CE, and add the functionality you need to optimize your workflow and processes. AniMove, XYTools and survey2gis are some of the plugins where we have contributed to recently.

    And, when needed, we will add the features you need to the core opensource tools. Some of our contributions are: coordinate transformations in GeoTools, GDAL PostGIS Raster driver, or Oracle Spatial support in 52north's SOS server.

    Customers: DEIMOS Space, Faunalia, CSGIS, ICC, Geosolutions, Port de Barcelona.

  • Web & Social Mapping

    Catalan Literary Map
    Bench Secrets

    The Catalan Literary Map allows you to explore and discover the links between territory and the literary expression of dozens of authors through texts, videos and tour proposals.

    Bench Secrets is the meeting point for bench lovers to discover Switzerland's most beautiful benches, leave messages for others and help document Switzerland's cultural heritage.

    Customers: Consorci del Besòs, Espais Escrits, Verein zur Förderung der Schweizer Bankkultur.
    Technologies: Django, Leaflet, Bootstrap, Spring, Postgis, Amazon AWS.
